Frequently Asked Questions

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

How does AI impact the job market?

AI has the potential to automate many tasks, leading to job displacement in some sectors. However, it also creates new opportunities and roles that require advanced technical skills.

Is AI safe?

While AI offers many benefits, it also poses risks such as ethical concerns, biases, and security vulnerabilities. It is important to implement responsible AI practices and policies to mitigate these risks.
