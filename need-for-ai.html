What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

Why We Need AI

  • Automation: AI automates repetitive tasks, increasing efficiency and allowing humans to focus on more complex problems.
  • Data Analysis: AI can process and analyze vast amounts of data faster than humans, providing valuable insights and aiding decision-making.
  • Healthcare: AI improves diagnostics, personalizes treatment, and predicts disease outbreaks.
  • Finance: AI enhances fraud detection, automates trading, and improves customer service.
  • Transportation: AI enables self-driving cars, optimizes traffic management, and enhances logistics.
